About

Bright Junior School is a primary school for both girls and boys. It favours both students who come from a further location and those who come from a near by location because it has both boarding and day section. The school is located in Wakiso Town Council at kasengejje road along side Hoima road near Majab Petrol Station.

What We Provide

Bright Junior School provides quality education to all students equally and leadership posts are also offered equally among all students.

We have quality teachers to give time to all learners such that all learners understand what they have been taught.

We also provide extra co-cirricular activities to learners such as aerobics sessions, football and others to provide refreshment to learners and help them get some rest.

Our history

Bright junior school was founded in 2010 by The late Kazibwe richard who was the headteaher and director by then. The School started with 15 students and had classes of Primary one to Priamry four. In 2012, the number of dtudents increased and classes of primary five and primary six were added to the school. In 2013, primary seven was added and it was the year bright junior school had its first candidates who sat for the Primary Leaving Exams(PLE) and they were 9 candidates who all got first grades
